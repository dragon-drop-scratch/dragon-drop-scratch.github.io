    • Do I need any HTML/CSS/JavaScript knowledge to make a website through DragonDrop?

    No, you don't need any coding language to use DragonDrop. If you do know some you can put it in to your website though.

    Do I need to pay to have Scratch tools on DragonDrop?

    No, you just need a Scratch account.

    Will the website I create go online the second I create it?

    Yes, the website will go online the instance you click that publish button. You just need to make sure that if you are planing on advertising your site on Scratch, have registered it.

    Will I have DragonDrop Ads on my website?

    There will be a small watermark, but if you have any Premium packages your site will be AD FREE!

    What are the differences between Free, Silver, Pro, and Premium Services?

    Silver and Pro Services are Premium Services. Premium Services are plans that are purchased, not
    free. Diffrent Premium Services come with diffrent features. Some Premium Services provide emails for your website, a free domain for one year, no ads,
    and many other features that the free plan does not include. The Pro plan contains all of
    what the Silver Plan covers, but more! The Silver Plan costs a one time payment of $10 (for as many sites as you wish), and Pro costs a one time fee of $50 (once again as many sites as you wish). To learn more see the full page about packages located here

    Do I get a free domain with the FREE service?

    When creating your site you will get four options. The three free options are to use a ".tk" domain, a ".dragondrops.net" domain, or to transfer a domain you alreay own over. The other option (you must have a pro plan for this) is to buy a domain from us! If you have a premium account you have one inculed for a year!

    Do I get a free email with the FREE service?

    No you will not. You must purchase Premium Services for email!
